About SlateMaker

BY MAKERS,
FOR MAKERS

SlateMaker started with a simple idea: the Slate EV pickup is built for people who love to build, modify, and make things their own. With its service-it-yourself philosophy and modular design, the Slate is practically begging for a community of makers to rally around it.

Based in Central Indiana — just a short drive from Slate's factory in Warsaw — we're building the go-to hub for open source designs, fabrication guides, and community knowledge. Whether you're 3D printing a custom phone mount or CNC-cutting a bed rack, this is your place.

We believe in open source by default, helping each other learn, and making the Slate ownership experience better for everyone.

What We Do

BUILD · MODIFY · SHARE

⚙

3D Printing

Download and share open source STL files for brackets, mounts, holders, covers, and accessories designed specifically for the Slate.

⚖

CNC & Fabrication

From laser-cut interior panels to CNC-milled bed accessories and welded bumpers — share your designs and build processes with the community.

⚡

Electrical & Wiring

Custom lighting, audio upgrades, wiring harnesses, and electrical mods. Document your work so others can learn and build on it.

🔧

Service & Repair

Slate is built to be serviced by you or your local shop. Share repair guides, maintenance tips, and service documentation.

📦

Design Repository

A growing open source library of maker-created designs, files, and documentation — version controlled and community reviewed.

🎓

Guides & Tutorials

Step-by-step build guides, video walkthroughs, and tutorials for every skill level — from your first 3D print to advanced fabrication.

The Platform

DESIGNED TO BE YOURS

The Slate EV pickup is built in Warsaw, Indiana with a radical idea: you should be able to modify, service, and truly own your vehicle. Its modular, bolt-on body panels and open service philosophy make it the perfect canvas for makers.
